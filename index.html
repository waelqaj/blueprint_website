APPAREL — DTG
Apparel

Direct to
Garment

Direct to Garment (DTG) printing is a modern digital method that applies water-based textile inks directly onto fabric using a specialized inkjet printer, reproducing full-color designs with precision and detail.

  • Full-color CMYK + white ink capability
  • No minimum order — 1 piece to 1,000+
  • Water-based eco-friendly inks
  • 1200 DPI resolution output
  • Best on 100% cotton or cotton blends
  • Soft hand feel — ink absorbs into fibers

APPAREL — DTF
Apparel

Direct to
Film

Direct to Film (DTF) printing prints designs onto specialized adhesive film before heat-transferring them onto fabric. Works on virtually any material — cotton, polyester, nylon, leather, and more.

  • Works on any fabric type or substrate
  • Full-color with fine details and gradients
  • Fade, crack, and peel resistant
  • Cost-effective at any quantity
  • Gang sheet layouts available

Who We Are

COMPANY COMPOSITION

Blueprint is a full-service creative agency based in the DC Metro Area. We partner with businesses of all sizes to craft identities, systems, and experiences built to last — not just designed to impress.

COMPOSITION: STRATEGY

Every project starts with deep thinking about your audience, competitors, and goals. Design without strategy is just decoration.

COMPOSITION: CRAFT

We obsess over the details — the right typeface, the perfect weight, the exact shade of blue. Craft is what separates good from great.

COMPOSITION: LONGEVITY

We don't chase trends. We build brands designed to look as sharp in 10 years as they do today.
